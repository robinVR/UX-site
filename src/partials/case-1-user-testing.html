User Testing

I tested the usability of my prototype with a pool of 10 engineers. Each user was asked to perform common documentation UI browsing and interaction tasks, their response was captured on video.

{{>hr}}

Through examination of the results we began to notice a behavior indicating difficulties in specific interface elements:.

  • Locating the version toggle button.
  • Difficulties in browsing and finding 4th level topics.
  • Minor difficulties in recognizing menu hierarchy.
  • Minor difficulties in finding the PDF version of a Document.
  • Minor difficulties in understanding content heading hierarchy.

In total 70% of users were able to complete the test without difficulty and perform all tests correctly.

With the usability issues discovered I made changes to the prototype.

Users had difficulty in finding the right hand version button.
